## What does ISRG do?
Intuitive Surgical makes the da Vinci surgical robot — the multi-armed system used in hospital ORs that lets a surgeon do minimally invasive surgery from a nearby console. It's a medical device / surgical robotics company, and its customers are hospitals all over the world (US, Europe, Japan, China). The real money isn't the robot itself — it's the disposable instruments, accessories, and service contracts that hospitals buy every time they use it (think Gillette: sell the razor once, sell the blades forever). Newer platforms include the da Vinci 5 (just rolling out) and Ion for lung biopsies.
