## What does ALAB do?
Astera Labs is a fabless semiconductor company that makes the connectivity chips inside modern AI data centers — PCIe retimers, CXL memory controllers, and smart cable modules. Think of them as the high-speed plumbing that lets thousands of GPUs, CPUs, and memory pools talk to each other inside a server rack without bottlenecks. Customers are the hyperscalers building AI infrastructure (Microsoft, Meta, Amazon, Google) plus NVIDIA's reference designs. The stock has been one of the breakout AI infra names of the cycle — up roughly 270% over the past year on insatiable demand from AI rack buildouts.
