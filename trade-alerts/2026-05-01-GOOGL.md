## What does GOOGL do?
GOOGL is Alphabet — the company behind Google search, YouTube, Android, Gmail, Google Maps, and Google Cloud. They make most of their money from advertising (search ads + YouTube ads), and the rest from cloud computing for businesses and a growing pile of AI products like the Gemini models and the Waymo self-driving fleet. Think of them as the front door to the internet for billions of people, plus one of the three big players in enterprise cloud (alongside Amazon and Microsoft). Their end customers range from every advertiser on earth to Fortune 500 companies running workloads on Google Cloud to anyone who's ever opened a Chrome tab.

## Why is this a good move right now?
The position has run hard — up 6x in about a month — and the chart is signaling that a cool-off / mean reversion is overdue. Selling the $385 call against half the position locks in cash today (~$15.20 per share) without forcing IA to sell any shares yet. The premium alone pays for roughly half of the original cost basis, so this leg is essentially "free" from here. Three ways it plays out: GOOGL drifts or pulls back → IA keeps the premium and the shares; GOOGL grinds higher but stays under $400.20 → still a win on both legs; GOOGL rips past $400.20 by June → upside above that level is capped on this half, but the other half rides free. That's the low-risk / high-reward setup — taking real money off the table on a parabolic run while still leaving room to keep winning.
