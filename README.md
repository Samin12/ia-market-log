# IA Market Log

A running log of trade alerts and market commentary from **InvestAnswers (IA)**, the Patreon-only research feed by James (bingo@patreon.com). Auto-populated from emails as they arrive.

## What's in here

- **`trade-alerts/`** — One file per IA Trade Alert (option sells, long entries, position closes). Filename pattern: `YYYY-MM-DD-TICKER.md`.
- **`market-insights/`** — One file per general market commentary email (macro, on-chain, BTC/ETH/SOL takes, sector notes, etc.). Filename pattern: `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`.

Both folders are append-only chronological logs. Newest entries sort to the bottom by date.

## Why this exists

Public, searchable archive of the signals and reasoning behind the trades — useful for backtesting one's own thinking against IA's track record over time.
